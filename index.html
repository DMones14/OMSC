Leading
the Way

With our commitment in providing excellent marine services to our stakeholders by conforming to maritime statutory requirements and international standards, OCEANWIDE leads the way as a reliable partner of the maritime industry.

The company is manned by enthusiastic young professionals with extensive experience, who served in varying capacities in the maritime industry, both locally and abroad, particularly in ship management, crew management, maritime training, maritime law, survey, audits, inspections and consultancy services. In our quest to pursue the institution’s commitment to be a strong, credible and reliable partner of the government in providing quality maritime services, Oceanwide have certified its services with the international standards of ISO 9001. Our accreditation as Recognized Security Organization (RSO) and MARSEC Training School of the Office for Transportation Security (OTS), MARPOL Surveying Organization of the Philippine Coast Guard (PCG), Maritime Training Institution (MTI) for both STCW and domestic training courses and Under Water Surveyor of Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA) is a manifestation of Oceanwide’s continued commitment to Quality Service and in offering one stop solution to its stakeholders.

About Us OCEANWIDE

Established Since 2012

Headquartered in Cebu, Philippines, our institution has evolved into a one-stop maritime solutions partner delivering effective and efficient services for compliance with statutory and customized maritime regulatory requirements. In 2017 we invested huge amount of resources, time and effort to expand the institution’s operation by opening a branch in Manila and Zamboanga respectively.

OCEANWIDE has been authorized by the different Philippine Administration Agencies who are tasked with the implementation of the different IMO conventions and domestic laws, to act on their behalf in conducting statutory surveys as well as audits and issuing the necessary statutory certificates. It is also an STCW-accredited maritime institution for the conduct of maritime training.

We carry out broad range of maritime services in the entire Philippines and the Asia Pacific. We cater to the needs of ship owners and other stakeholders of the maritime industry. Using technology and a team of highly qualified naval architects, marine engineers, master mariners, other engineers, instructors, assessors, supervisors and technical personnel, we give you tailor made services and solutions to cater your organization’s needs. Guided by company code of ethics, we provide uncompromising commitment to quality maritime services and outcomes based training geared towards high impact learning. Offering maritime solutions servicestogether with its affiliates and sister companies in the Oceanwide Group. We commit at designing and proper conduct of up-to-date outcomesbased training programs that meet not only the minimum standards set by the Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping for Seafarers (STCW Convention) but most especially the standards set by the world maritime industry and its stakeholders.

We treat our clients as important partners and provide them with incomparable schedule, flexibility and personalized services without compromising confidentiality and integrity.

Services & Expertise

🏢 Administration's Accredited Services

Maritime Security (MARSEC) Training School

As an accredited Maritime Security (MARSEC) Training School of the Philippine Administration, the Office for Transportation Security (OTS) against the STCW Convention and the ISPS Code, for the conduct of different MARSEC training courses required for seafarers and port facility personnel.

Recognized/Registered Security Organization (RSO/ReSo)

As a Recognized/Registered Security Organization (RSO/ReSO) of the Philippine Administration for ISPS Code, the Office for Transportation Security (OTS), we carry out audit of ship and ports for the issuance of National Ship Security Certificate (NSSC) and Statement of Compliance of a Port Facility (SoCPF), respectively.

Marpol Survey Organization (MSO)

As a Marpol Survey Organization (MSO) of the Philippine Administration for MARPOL Convention, the Philippine Coast Guard (PCG), we carry out survey of ships for the issuance of Oil Pollution Prevention Certificate (OPPC) and Sewage Pollution Prevention Certificate (SPPC).

Accredited Underwater Surveyor

As an accredited Underwater Surveyor of the Philippine Administration, the Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA), we carry out underwater inspection/survey of the ship’s integral parts which are below waterline while the ship is in afloat position for the issuance of a Certificate of Inspection (CI) after complying with the PMMRR ’97 and other safety rules issued subsequently by MARINA as well as Interim Certificate of Inspection (ICI) to extend the validity of Certificate of Inspection (CI) after a favorable result of the underwater survey

Maritime Training Institution (MTI)

As an accredited Maritime Training Institution (MTI) of the Philippine Administration, the Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA) against the Standards of Training, Certificate and Watchkeeping (STCW), for the conduct of different training courses required for Philippine seafarers to become internationally competent professionals.

OCEANWIDE Group also provide tailor-made services to cater to the customers' specific requirements, in the fields of Naval Architecture, Marine Engineering, Management Systems, Risk and Reliability and Maritime Law.

Survey & Certification

As a Recognized/Registered Security Organization of the Administration, OCEANWIDE is authorized to carry out the following services on behalf of the administration.

01

MARPOL Survey

For the issuance and endorsement of:

  • Oil Pollution Prevention Certificate (OPPC)
  • Sewage Pollution Prevention Certificate (SPPC)

for Philippine Registered Ships.

02

MARSEC Audit

For the issuance and endorsement of:

  • National Ship Security Certificate (NSSC)
  • Statement of Compliance of a Port Facility (SoCPF)

for Philippine Registered Ships and Ports, respectively.

03

Underwater Survey

Of Philippine Registered Ships for the issuance of a Certificate of Inspection or to extend the validity of Certificate of Inspection through underwater survey of the ship's integral parts which are below waterline.
